The Audi Q5

Choose from a range of highly efficient turbocharged petrol or diesel engines, including the new 2.0 TFSI quattro with 185 kW of power and 370 Nm of torque. With Audi drive select standard across the Q5 range, you can tailor the performance characteristics at the touch of a button to suit your driving preference.

Exterior

Every detail of the Q5 is characterised by the typical Audi Q model design. With its broad stance, sharper, more aggressive lines, and striking LED daytime running lights, it has a confident bearing and an elegant sports-inspired aesthetic. The sensor controlled electric tailgate takes convenience to another level. Simply move your foot beneath the rear bumper to open or close the rear tailgate, without having to search for the key.

  • quattro with ultra technology

    A pioneer in all-wheel drive technology, Audi developed the revolutionary quattro all-wheel drive – a system that delivers superb handling and traction for enhanced stability on wet or low grip surfaces. The Q5 brings an evolution of quattro by incorporating new ultra technology, designed to disengage the rear-axle drive whenever it’s not needed, and proactively re-engage it when necessary. This new concept boosts efficiency without compromising traction or driving dynamics.


  • Audi virtual cockpit

    Standard on Sport models, the Audi virtual cockpit is a 12.3 inch digital instrument cluster that replaces traditional dials. Driver information, navigation data and infotainment functions are displayed in brilliant high resolution, with two different layouts possible at the touch of a button – progressive and classic.


  • Class-leading safety technology

    The Q5 takes safety very seriously. It boasts a raft of advanced safety technology dedicated to keeping you – and everyone around you – safe from harm. Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) with pedestrian detection, rear cross traffic alert, side assist, pre-sense rear and exit warning system are standard across the Q5 model range.

Interior

The sophisticated interior exudes luxury, with leather appointed seats and contemporary, ambient lighting that enhances the clean lines. The larger cabin provides more space for both seating and luggage, while the 3 zone deluxe automatic air conditioning offers all passengers even more comfort. The slimline colour infotainment screen offers impressive clarity for MMI navigation, Audi smartphone interface, digital radio and Audi connect.

Utilising the latest factory-specific tools, Audi dealers have a wealth of knowledge and direct access to the latest software and technical product information to keep your Audi in peak condition.
